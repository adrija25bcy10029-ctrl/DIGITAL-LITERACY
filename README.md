# DIGITAL-LITERACY
#TASK1
For this task, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva. The goal of the design was to present important aspects of digital literacy in a simple and visually appealing way. My infographic included key topics such as the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette.

While designing the infographic, I focused on keeping the content clear and concise by using short bullet points and relevant icons. I also used a consistent color theme and layout to make the information easy to understand at a glance. Canva provided a variety of templates and design elements, which made the process smooth and creative.

One challenge I faced was selecting the right amount of information without overcrowding the design. It was important to balance visuals and text so that the infographic remained attractive and readable. Overall, this task helped me improve my ability to present information visually and understand the importance of digital literacy in everyday student life.
